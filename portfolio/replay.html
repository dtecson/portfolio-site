Overview

The main objective of this application was to serve as a geospatial tool with DVR-like capabilities. It had the ability to pull in and visualize muiltiple feeds simultaneously, giving users improved situational awareness. Initial target users were in the military space, but it has evolved to be used in the commercial space. It was the base product for many of the projects I worked on.

What I worked on

Based on a plugin architecture, I worked on many of the base features and interactions of the product, including the ability to import files, subscribing and managing data feeds and map layers, as well as managing user settings. As the lead UX, it was also my responsibility to oversee all the features to ensure a consistent experience throughout the application. Some additional features are focused on in other parts of my portfolio.
