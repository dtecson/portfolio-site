      Overview

      The client for this project needed to have discussions with various groups and individuals concerning very complex systems. Since many of the people they talked with had no prior knowledge of the systems, they needed a tool that would allow them to get others up to speed quickly.

      What I worked on

      I was involved with the initial discussions, research, information architecture or the project. We reviewed the clients current process and attempted to understand the complex systems as best we could. Due to confidential nature of the project and it's information, we were not allowed access to the actual data that would be presented, which made for a challenging project. I assisted in the interaction design, collaborating the designer on ideas and concepts. Later in the project, I worked on the data entry tool for the project.
